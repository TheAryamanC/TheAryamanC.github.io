2048: Half-life

This was one of the first games I ever worked on. It is a variant of the classic game 2048, with a twist.

The tiles decay over time if they aren't merged, which adds an extra layer of strategy to the gameplay.

You can play it at https://thearyamanc.github.io/2048 .

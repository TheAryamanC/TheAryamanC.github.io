MKULTRA

MKULTRA is an experimental AI game by Prof. Ian Horswill. The project was designed to test advanced symbolic reasoning and natural language processing in non-player characters (NPCs).

Here are some AI techniques incorporated:

 1. Belief Injection: Players can directly manipulate NPCs by rewriting their underlying data and inserting false beliefs into their knowledge bases to force the character to alter their behavior based on the new "fact".

 2. Natural Language Processing: All the hype today, for some reason, but using Logic Programming instead of more recent machine learning approaches.

 3. Reactive Planning: Characters execute tasks using a reactive planner that controls their physical actions in the game.

My work was mostly updating legacy systems to make the game playable. At the moment, Ian and I are the only ones that can actually play this game, probably because most users don't fully understand how to actually perform actions within the game, and it remains to be seen if someone can improve the NLP capabilities of the characters without resorting to calling LLMs.
